RTT TERMILOLOGY AND NATIONAL CODES MEANINGS

RTT – Referral to Treatment

PTL – Patient Tracking List – a list of patients who need to be treated by given dates

Nullify – To cause something to have no value or effect

DNA – Did Not Attend (e.g. OP appointment)

Patient Cancellation – Activity cancelled by the patient

Hospital Cancellation – Activity cancelled by the Hospital

IPTMDS – Inter Provider Administrative Data Transfer Minimum Data Set

Ad-hoc - Administrative events that happen outside hospital setting

PBL- PARTIAL BOOKING LIST - LIST OF PATIENT WAITING FOR FIRST APPOINTMENT (OPA)

OPA/OPD OUTPATIENT APPOINTMENT

WL- WAITING LIST (FOR PATIENT WHO ARE ADDED TO LIST TO HAVE DIAGNOSTIC TEST OR TREATMENT

TCI DATE- TO COME IN DATE ( DATE FOR SURGERY OR DIAGNOSTIC)

WL ACTIVE- WAITING LIST THAT IS ACTIVE

PL- PLANNED WAITING LIST

OP REG DATE- OUTPATIENT REGISTRATION DATE

OP DISCHARGED DATE - OUT PATIENT DISCHARGED DATE

N- NEW APPOINTMENT

F- FOLLOW UP APPOINTMENT

PATHWAY NUMBER: UNIQUE NUMBER TO IDENTIFY EACH PATHWAY OR FOLDERS OPEN FOR THE PATIENT

REFERRAL TO TREATMENT PERIOD START DATE- CLOCK START DATE

REFERRAL TO TREATMENT PERIOD END DATE- CLOCK END/STOP DATE

RTT NATIONAL STATUS CODES AND THEIR MEANINGS

10 - The FIRST ACTIVITY in a pathway

11 - The FIRST ACTIVITY AFTER ACTIVE MONITORING END/ OR WATCHFUL WAIT ENDS

12 - FIRST ACTIVITY following a CONSULTANT or AHP REFERRAL FOR A NEW CONDITION

20 - SUBSEQUENT ACTIVITY in an RTT period

21 - Transfer to ANOTHER HEALTH CARE PROVIDER

The Code below Stops a clock or RTT period

30 - Start of FIRST DEFINITIVE TREATMENT

31 - Start of ACTIVE MONITORING initiated by the PATIENT

32 - Start of ACTIVE MONITORING initiated by the HOSPITAL

33 - DNA – PATIENT Did Not Attend the FIRST CARE ACTIVITY

34 - Decision NOT TO TREAT or NO FURTHER contact required

35 - PATIENT declined offered treatment

36 - PATIENT died before treatment

90 - First Definitive Treatment OCCURRED PREVIOUSLY

91 - Care activity DURING Active Monitoring

Non RTT activity

92 - DIAGNOSTICS ONLY (not yet referred for treatment)

98 - Activity NOT APPLICABLE to RTT PERIODS
